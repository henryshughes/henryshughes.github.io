About Me

I'm a Civil Engineering student at the University of Waterloo

In my career I would love to use technology to help develop and maintain the infrastructure that we need to live out our daily lives. The building and maintenance of sustainable infrasturcture will be critical as we continue in our battle against global warming, which is one of the reasons I am so interested in this sector.
