About Me

I'm a Civil Engineering student at the University of Waterloo

In my career I am looking to use technology to help develop and maintain the infrastructure that we need to live out our daily lives. I am currently researching the applications of machine learning in hydrology at the University of Waterloo, and am looking to continue doing this at the graduate level.

I am very interested in both technology and infrastructure. I would love to chat with anyone who has an interest in either of these sectors, so please feel free to reach out!
